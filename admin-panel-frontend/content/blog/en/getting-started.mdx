# Getting Started with Appuri Hogosha

**Appuri Hogosha** is a school notification platform that helps teachers and administrators communicate with parents quickly and efficiently.

## What You Can Do

- 📢 Send instant push notifications to parents
- 📋 Manage student and parent records
- 👥 Organize students into groups
- 📱 Reach parents via mobile app or SMS

## Step 1: Log In to the Admin Panel

Open the admin panel in your browser and log in with your school administrator credentials. If you do not have an account yet, contact your system administrator.

## Step 2: Add Students and Parents

Navigate to **Students** in the sidebar. Click **Add Student** and fill in the student's name, class, and the parent's contact details. Each parent will receive an invitation to install the Appuri Hogosha mobile app.

## Step 3: Create Groups

Groups let you send targeted notifications. Go to **Groups**, create a class group (e.g., "Grade 5A"), and assign students to it.

## Step 4: Send Your First Notification

Go to **Messages** and click **New Message**. Select a recipient group or individual parents, write your message, and tap **Send**. Parents receive a push notification on their phone within seconds.

## Tips for Effective Notifications

1. **Be concise** — parents read messages quickly on their phone.
2. **Use groups** — avoid sending irrelevant messages to all parents.
3. **Schedule messages** — use the date/time picker for planned announcements.

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues, please contact your school's system administrator or visit the [parent notification page](/parentnotification) to check app availability.
